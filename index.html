RECIPES

Rajma chawal is an indian popular meal its a combination of rice and kidney beans with spicy gravy

INGREDIENTS FOR RAJMA CHAWAL

  • BASMATI RICE
  • SOAKED RICE
  • REFINED OIL
  • CHOPPED ONION
  • TOMATO PUIRY
  • CHOPPED GARLIC,CHILI
  • GINGER-GARLIC PASTE
  • MASALE:
  • GARAM MASALA
  • CHILI POWDER
  • SALT TO TASTE
  • KASURI METHI

STEPS TO MAKE IT :

  1. boil soaked rajma beans.
  2. take some oil in pan.
  3. heat the pan.
  4. add chopped and chilli.
  5. fry until it become golden brown.
  6. add GINGER-GARLIC paste in it.
  7. cool down this fried batter grind this batter in mixer.
  8. take this grinded batter in pan again and add butter and add kasuri methi.
  9. boil rice until it become soft
  10. serve and enjoy your favorite rajma chawal 😜.
